Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the VisualKey Service and tells you about your privacy rights. The Service is provided by the project creator ("the Creator", "We", "Us", or "Our").

Data Handling and Your Privacy

Private Key Generation and Usage

The core tools of the Service, such as the "Visualizer Tool", "Find Rare Keys", and "Vanity Address Generator", create Ethereum private keys and addresses. This entire process is performed exclusively in your browser on your local machine.

  • We never see, store, or transmit your private keys. They are generated and exist only on your computer.
  • To improve user experience when moving between pages (e.g., from finding a rare key to minting it), the generated private key may be temporarily stored in your browser's `sessionStorage`. This storage is local to your browser and is automatically cleared when you close the browser tab.
  • You are solely responsible for the secure management of any private keys you generate and choose to save.

Blockchain Interactions

For on-chain transactions like buying, burning, or minting tokens, the Service helps you interact with your own third-party wallet (e.g., MetaMask).

  • Your private keys are managed securely by your wallet provider and are never exposed to our Service.
  • Your public wallet address and all transaction details are permanently recorded on the public blockchain. We do not control this public data.

Usage Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data to understand how users interact with the Service. This helps us improve features and user experience. This data may include:

  • Your device's IP address, browser type, and operating system.
  • The pages you visit and the time you spend on them.
  • The features you use and the buttons you click.

This information is aggregated and does not personally identify you. We do not use this data for advertising. You can opt-out of Google Analytics tracking by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with Personal Data, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected Personal Data from a child under 13 without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information.

Security

We are committed to ensuring the security of our Service. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
